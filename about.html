About

A small digital recipe book — designed as an academic project and a personal archive.

Where it all started


Bake&Cake was my first job. I started this project at 15 years old and learned, through practice, how to manage a brand in a comprehensive way — from graphic design to communication and content organization.

Six years later, with more experience and stronger design tools, I decided to revisit the project and transform it into a digital recipe book — a way to carry it with me over time and give new meaning to the place where my design journey began.
