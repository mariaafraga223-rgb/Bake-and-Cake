White Chocolate Pistachio Cookies

Sweet white chocolate, crunchy pistachios, and an easy slice-and-bake format — clean, elegant, and perfect for make-ahead baking.

Prep 20 min | Cook 10 min | Total 30 min | Servings 24 cookies

Ingredients


  • 170 g unsalted butter (room temperature)
  • 100 g light brown sugar (packed)
  • 50 g granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 219 g all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 227 g unsalted pistachios, finely chopped (divided)
  • 80 g dried cranberries, roughly chopped
  • 113 g white chocolate, finely chopped

Steps


  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C and line two baking trays with parchment paper.
  2. Beat the butter with the brown sugar and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the egg yolk and vanilla extract, mixing until smooth.
  4. Add the flour and salt and mix just until combined (do not overmix).
  5. Fold in half of the chopped pistachios, the cranberries, and the white chocolate.
  6. Divide the dough in two. Roll each half into a log about 30 cm long.
  7. Roll the logs in the remaining pistachios, pressing gently so they stick. Wrap tightly and chill until firm (about 2 hours).
  8. Slice into thin rounds and place on the prepared trays, leaving space between cookies.
  9. Bake for about 10 minutes, until the edges are lightly golden. Cool completely before serving.
