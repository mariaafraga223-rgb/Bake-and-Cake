Red Velvet Cookies

Soft red velvet cookies with a creamy cheesecake center — rich, chocolatey, and slightly tangy.

Prep 20 min | Cook 14–16 min | Total 1 hr 40 min | Servings 12 cookies

Ingredients


Cheesecake center
  • 170 g cream cheese (room temperature)
  • 60 g powdered sugar
  • 16 g all-purpose flour (2 tbsp)
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Cookies
  • 114 g unsalted butter (room temperature)
  • 100 g light brown sugar
  • 100 g granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg + 1 large egg yolk (room temperature)
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp red gel food coloring
  • 29 g Dutch-processed cocoa powder
  • 219 g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 100 g granulated sugar (optional, for rolling)

Steps


  1. Make the cheesecake center: Mix cream cheese, powdered sugar, flour, and vanilla until smooth.
  2. Pipe or spoon small discs (about 2 tsp each) onto a parchment-lined tray and freeze until firm (at least 1 hour).
  3. Preheat the oven to 180°C and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  4. Beat butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  5. Add the egg, egg yolk, and vanilla. Mix, then add the red food coloring.
  6. Add cocoa powder, flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix just until combined.
  7. Scoop dough portions and flatten each slightly. Place a frozen cheesecake disc in the center.
  8. Wrap the dough around the filling and pinch to seal into a ball.
  9. If using, roll dough balls in granulated sugar. Place on the tray with space between.
  10. Bake for 14–16 minutes, until set on the edges but still gooey in the middle.
  11. Cool slightly on the tray, then transfer to a rack to cool completely.
