Lemon Blueberry Cookies

Bright lemon flavor, blueberry pops, and a soft bakery-style bite — fresh, light, and a little sweet.

Prep 15 min | Cook 12–15 min | Total 30 min | Servings 12 cookies

Ingredients


  • 114 g unsalted butter (room temperature)
  • 100 g light brown sugar (packed)
  • 50 g granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg (room temperature)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp lemon extract
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • 188 g all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 74 g frozen blueberries
  • 80 g blueberry preserves
  • 113 g white chocolate, roughly chopped

Steps


  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line two baking trays with parchment paper.
  2. Beat the butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the egg, vanilla extract, lemon extract, and lemon zest. Mix until smooth.
  4. Add the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix just until combined.
  5. Gently fold in the frozen blueberries and white chocolate.
  6. Add the blueberry preserves in spoonfuls and fold only 1–2 times (keep visible ripples).
  7. Scoop dough portions onto the trays, leaving space between cookies.
  8. Bake for 12–15 minutes, until set on the edges but still soft in the middle.
  9. Let rest on the tray for 5 minutes, then transfer to a rack to cool completely.
