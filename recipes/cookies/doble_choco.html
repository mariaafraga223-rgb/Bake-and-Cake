Double Chocolate Cookies

Rich, soft and deeply chocolatey — made for true chocolate lovers.

Prep 15 min | Cook 11 min | Total 26 min | Servings 12 cookies

Ingredients


  • 114 g unsalted butter
  • 200 g brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 29 g cocoa powder
  • 125 g all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 224 g chocolate chips

Steps


  1. Preheat oven to 180 °C.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Add egg and vanilla.
  4. Mix cocoa, flour, baking soda and salt.
  5. Combine dry ingredients with wet.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips.
  7. Bake for 11 minutes.
