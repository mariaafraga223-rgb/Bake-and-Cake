Nutella Brownies

Ultra-fudgy brownies with thick ribbons of Nutella swirled through — rich, glossy, and indulgent.

Prep 15 min | Cook 30 min | Total 45 min | Servings 9–16 brownies

Ingredients


  • 114 g unsalted butter
  • 133 g granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 363 g Nutella + about 90 g for swirling
  • 94 g all-purpose flour
  • 22 g Dutch-process cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps


  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a 20 × 20 cm baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine butter and sugar. Microwave for 30 seconds, stir, and repeat twice until glossy.
  3. Let the mixture cool slightly, then whisk in eggs one at a time until smooth and lighter in color.
  4. Add 363 g Nutella and vanilla extract. Mix until fully combined.
  5. Fold in cocoa powder, flour, and salt until just combined (do not overmix).
  6. Spread the batter evenly into the prepared pan.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of the remaining Nutella over the surface and swirl gently with a knife to create thick ribbons.
  8. Bake for about 30 minutes, until edges are set but a knife inserted in the center comes out with a bit of moist batter.
  9. Allow to cool completely before slicing into 9 large or 16 smaller brownies.
