Reese’s Brownies

Fudgy cocoa brownies packed with peanut butter cup pieces — rich, gooey, and impossible to share.

Prep 10 min | Cook 35 min | Total 45 min | Servings 9 squares

Ingredients


  • 142 g unsalted butter (about 1/2 cup + 2 tbsp)
  • 275 g light brown sugar (about 1 1/4 cups, packed)
  • 2 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 64 g cocoa powder (about 3/4 cup)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 94 g all-purpose flour
  • About 110–120 g peanut butter cups, roughly chopped (about 2/3 cup)

Steps


  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a 20 × 20 cm pan with parchment paper (and lightly grease if you want).
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine butter and brown sugar. Microwave 30 seconds, stir; repeat 2 more times (you’re aiming for a glossy mixture).
  3. Let cool slightly, then add eggs one at a time, whisking between each addition. Whisk until the mixture lightens a bit.
  4. Add cocoa powder, salt, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
  5. Fold in flour until just combined (don’t overmix).
  6. Gently fold in the chopped peanut butter cups.
  7. Spread batter evenly into the prepared pan.
  8. Bake about 35 minutes. Start checking at 30 minutes: edges should be set and a knife in the center should come out with a bit of moist batter/crumbs.
  9. Cool in the pan before slicing into squares and serving.
