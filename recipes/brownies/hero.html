Better Than Boxed Brownies

Rich, fudgy and deeply chocolatey. These brownies deliver everything you expect from a boxed mix — but better.

Prep 15 min | Cook 25 min | Total 45 min | Servings 9 brownies

Ingredients


  • 114 g unsalted butter
  • 250 g granulated sugar
  • 113 g bittersweet chocolate, finely chopped
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 94 g all-purpose flour
  • 22 g Dutch-process cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 74 g bittersweet chocolate chips (optional)
  • 56 g semisweet chocolate chips (optional)

Steps


  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a 20 × 20 cm baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine butter and sugar. Microwave for about 1 minute until melted and hot. Whisk.
  3. Add the chopped chocolate and microwave 30 seconds more. Stir until fully melted and smooth.
  4. Add eggs straight from the fridge and vanilla extract. Whisk vigorously for about 30 seconds until glossy.
  5. Add flour, cocoa powder and salt. Whisk slowly until just combined.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips if using.
  7. Pour batter into the prepared pan and spread evenly.
  8. Bake for 20–25 minutes, until edges are set and the center has moist crumbs.
  9. Cool completely in the pan, then lift out, slice into squares and serve.
