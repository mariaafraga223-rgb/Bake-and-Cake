Lemonies

Bright, soft lemon bars with a glossy glaze — sweet, zesty, and perfectly simple.

Prep 30 min | Cook 20–25 min | Total 1 hr | Servings 9 squares

Ingredients


For the lemonies
  • 200 g granulated sugar
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 114 g unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp lemon extract
  • 125 g all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

For the lemon glaze
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 60 g powdered sugar
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Steps


  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a 20 × 20 cm baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine sugar and lemon zest. Rub together with your fingers until fragrant.
  3. Add butter and microwave about 1 minute, until melted and hot. Whisk well (microwave 15 seconds more if needed).
  4. Add eggs, vanilla extract, and lemon extract. Whisk vigorously for about 30 seconds until glossy and lighter.
  5. Add flour, salt, and baking powder. Fold gently until just combined (do not overmix).
  6. Spread batter evenly into the prepared pan.
  7. Bake 20–25 minutes, until edges are set and the center tests mostly clean. Cool completely.
  8. Make the glaze: whisk lemon juice, powdered sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  9. Spread glaze over cooled lemonies. Let set, then slice into squares and serve.
