Pumpkin Carrot Cake Muffins

Cozy, spiced, and moist — a carrot-cake style muffin with pumpkin and warm spices (plus an optional glaze).

Prep 60 min | Cook 30 min | Total 1 hr 30 min | Servings 12 muffins

Ingredients


  • 96 g freshly shredded carrots
  • 123 g pumpkin puree
  • 80 g sweetened shredded coconut
  • 41 g raisins
  • 150 g granulated sugar
  • 79 ml vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs (room temperature)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 125 g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps


  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C and line a 12-cup muffin tin with liners.
  2. In a large bowl, combine carrots, pumpkin puree, coconut, raisins, sugar, oil, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and salt.
  4. Add dry ingredients to the wet mixture and stir until just combined (don’t overmix).
  5. Divide batter evenly between the 12 muffin cups (about 3/4 full).
  6. Bake 5 minutes at 220°C, then reduce to 190°C and bake 15 minutes more, until a knife comes out mostly clean.
  7. Cool muffins slightly. If using glaze, whisk all glaze ingredients until smooth and drizzle over warm muffins.
