Lemon Poppy Seed Muffins

Bright lemon flavor with poppy seeds in every bite — soft, fluffy muffins with a fresh citrus finish.

Prep 10 min | Cook 16 min | Total 26 min | Servings 10 muffins

Ingredients


  • 250 g granulated sugar
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 114 g unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 eggs (room temperature)
  • 59 ml milk
  • 58 g sour cream (or Greek yogurt)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 250 g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp poppy seeds

Steps


  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C. Grease and line 10 standard muffin cups with liners.
  2. In a large bowl, combine sugar and lemon zest. Rub together with your fingers until fragrant.
  3. Add melted butter and mix until combined.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing between each until smooth.
  5. Add sour cream, milk, lemon juice, and vanilla. Mix well.
  6. Add flour, baking powder, and salt. Mix until just combined and no flour streaks remain.
  7. Fold in poppy seeds until evenly distributed (don’t overmix).
  8. Scoop batter into muffin cups, filling about 3/4 full.
  9. Bake 5 minutes at 220°C, then reduce to 190°C and bake 12 minutes more, until golden and springy.
  10. Cool at least 15 minutes before eating.
