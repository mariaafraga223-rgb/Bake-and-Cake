Banana & Nutella Muffins

Extra-ripe bananas, a soft crumb, and a generous Nutella swirl — simple, cozy, and indulgent.

Prep 1 hr | Cook 15–25 min | Total 1 hr 30 min | Servings 12 muffins

Ingredients


  • 3 extremely ripe bananas, mashed
  • 85 g unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 eggs (room temperature)
  • 170 g nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 250 g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 150 g sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 363 g Nutella

Steps


  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Grease 12 standard muffin cups (or 6 jumbo) and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, mix mashed bananas with melted butter.
  3. Add eggs, Greek yogurt, and vanilla. Mix until combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, salt, and sugar.
  5. Fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until just combined (don’t overmix).
  6. Divide batter evenly between muffin cups.
  7. Warm Nutella in the microwave for about 20 seconds to loosen.
  8. Drizzle Nutella over each muffin and gently swirl with a knife.
  9. Bake until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean: 15–20 minutes for standard muffins, 20–25 minutes for jumbo.
  10. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
