Banana Bread

Moist, naturally sweet banana bread made with ripe bananas and a tender crumb — simple and comforting.

Prep 10 min | Cook 45–60 min | Total 1 h | Servings 1 loaf

Ingredients


  • 73 g coconut oil, melted
  • 3 overripe bananas, mashed
  • 113 g coconut sugar
  • 2 eggs (room temperature)
  • 120 g nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 148 g gluten-free flour (or all-purpose flour)
  • 13 g almond meal
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps


  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a standard 23 cm (9-inch) loaf pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, mash the bananas until smooth with no large chunks.
  3. Add coconut sugar, eggs, melted coconut oil, yogurt, and vanilla. Mix until well combined.
  4. Add flour, almond meal, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Mix gently until just combined and no streaks of flour remain.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 45–60 minutes, until a knife inserted into the center comes out mostly clean.
  8. Allow to cool before slicing and serving.
