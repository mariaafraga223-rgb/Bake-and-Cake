Lemon Loaf

Bright, soft lemon loaf with a simple glaze — a clean, café-style classic.

Prep 15 min | Cook 45–60 min | Total 1 h | Servings 1 loaf

Ingredients


  • 200 g granulated sugar
  • Zest of 2 lemons (about 2 tbsp)
  • 114 g unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 eggs (room temperature)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 58 g sour cream
  • 59 ml milk
  • 208 g all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder

Lemon glaze
  • 180 g powdered sugar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp milk or cream
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps


  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a 1 lb loaf pan with parchment paper or grease well.
  2. In a large bowl, rub the lemon zest into the sugar using your fingers until fragrant.
  3. Add melted butter and mix well. Add vanilla, then add eggs one at a time, mixing between each addition.
  4. Add sour cream, milk, and lemon juice. Mix to combine.
  5. Add flour, baking powder, and salt. Mix until smooth and no lumps remain (don’t overmix).
  6. Pour batter into the loaf pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake 45–60 minutes, until a knife inserted in the center comes out mostly clean.
  8. Make the glaze: whisk powdered sugar, lemon juice, milk/cream, melted butter, salt, and vanilla until smooth.
  9. Once the loaf is cool, remove from the pan and pour glaze over the top. Let set, then slice.
