Cardamom Banana Loaf

Moist banana bread with warm cardamom-ginger spice and a crunchy streusel topping — cozy and aromatic.

Prep 15 min | Cook 45 min | Total 1 h | Servings 1 loaf

Ingredients


For the loaf
  • 3 very ripe bananas, mashed
  • 85 g unsalted butter, melted
  • 50 g granulated sugar
  • 100 g light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 190 g Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 250 g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 3/4 tsp salt

For the streusel
  • 1/2 cup cashews, finely chopped
  • 50 g granulated sugar
  • 31 g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  • 45 g unsalted butter, melted

Steps


  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a loaf pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, mix mashed bananas with melted butter.
  3. Add granulated sugar, brown sugar, eggs, Greek yogurt and vanilla. Mix until combined.
  4. Add flour, baking soda, cardamom, ginger and salt. Fold until just combined.
  5. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  6. Make the streusel: mix cashews, sugar, flour, cardamom and ginger. Add melted butter and rub together until crumbly.
  7. Sprinkle streusel evenly over the batter, covering the top.
  8. Bake for about 45 minutes, until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
  9. If the streusel browns too fast, loosely cover with foil after 30 minutes.
  10. Cool before slicing for best texture.
