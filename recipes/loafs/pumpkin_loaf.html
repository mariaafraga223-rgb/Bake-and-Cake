Pumpkin & Cream Cheese Loaf

Spiced pumpkin bread with thick ribbons of creamy cheesecake — cozy, soft and perfectly balanced.

Prep 15 min | Cook 45 min | Total 1 h | Servings 1 loaf

Ingredients


For the pumpkin loaf
  • 188 g all-purpose flour
  • 150 g granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  • 2 large eggs
  • 245 g pumpkin purée
  • 120 ml vegetable oil
  • 80 ml milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

For the cream cheese swirl
  • 227 g cream cheese, room temperature
  • 50 g granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg, room temperature
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps


  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a loaf pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, salt, nutmeg, cinnamon and cloves.
  3. Make a well in the center and add eggs, pumpkin purée, oil, milk and vanilla.
  4. Whisk briefly, then fold until just combined.
  5. Pour half of the pumpkin batter into the prepared pan and set the rest aside.
  6. In a separate bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, egg, flour and vanilla until smooth.
  7. Spoon the cream cheese mixture over the pumpkin batter.
  8. Pour remaining pumpkin batter on top.
  9. Use a knife to gently swirl — do not overmix.
  10. Bake for about 45 minutes, until a knife inserted comes out mostly clean.
  11. Cool completely before slicing for clean layers.
