Zucchini Loaf

Soft, spiced and extra moist — zucchini + pineapple make this loaf tender for days, finished with a crunchy walnut streusel.

Prep 1 h | Cook 50–60 min | Total 1 h 50 min | Servings 1 loaf

Ingredients


For the loaf
  • 120 ml vegetable oil
  • 200 g granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 113 g crushed pineapple, drained
  • 260 g grated zucchini, pressed
  • 281 g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp salt

For the streusel
  • 60 g walnuts, chopped
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 42 g all-purpose flour
  • 110 g brown sugar
  • 57 g butter, cold and cubed

Steps


  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a loaf pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, beat the oil and granulated sugar for about 1 minute.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla and beat for another minute.
  4. Stir in drained pineapple and grated zucchini.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt.
  6. Fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until just combined.
  7. Make the streusel: pulse walnuts, cinnamon, flour and brown sugar, then pulse in the cold butter until crumbly.
  8. Pour half of the batter into the pan, then sprinkle half of the streusel on top (pinch it into clumps as you sprinkle).
  9. Add remaining batter and top with the rest of the streusel.
  10. Bake for about 50 minutes, or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
  11. Cool slightly, then lift out of the pan and cool completely before slicing.
