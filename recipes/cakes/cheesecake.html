Cheesecake

Creamy, smooth and classic — a timeless cheesecake with a buttery graham cracker crust.

Prep 30 min | Cook 55 min | Total 4 h 30 min | Servings 16 slices

Ingredients


Graham cracker crust
  • 180 g graham cracker crumbs
  • 114 g unsalted butter, melted
  • 65 g granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Cheesecake filling
  • 680 g cream cheese, room temperature
  • 227 g sour cream, room temperature
  • 250 g granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract

Steps


  1. Preheat the oven to 165°C. Place a baking dish filled with hot water on the bottom rack to create steam.
  2. Line a 23 cm springform pan with parchment paper and lightly grease the sides.
  3. Make the crust: mix graham crumbs, melted butter, sugar and salt until sandy.
  4. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of the pan. Set aside.
  5. In a mixer, beat cream cheese, sour cream and sugar on low speed until smooth.
  6. Add eggs one at a time, mixing gently. Add vanilla and mix until just combined.
  7. Pour the filling over the crust. Tap the pan gently to release air bubbles.
  8. Bake for about 55 minutes, until edges are set but the center still jiggles slightly.
  9. Turn off the oven, crack the door open and leave cheesecake inside for 1 hour.
  10. Remove, run a knife around the edges and cool at room temperature for 1 hour.
  11. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours (or overnight) before serving.
