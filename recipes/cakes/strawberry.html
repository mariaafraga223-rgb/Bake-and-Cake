Strawberry Shortcake

Light vanilla cake topped with whipped cream and fresh strawberries — simple, airy and timeless.

Prep 30 min | Cook 30 min | Total 1 h 30 min | Servings 8 slices

Ingredients


Cake
  • 250 g all-purpose flour
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 200 g granulated sugar
  • 114 g unsalted butter, cold
  • 237 ml milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Demerara sugar (optional, for topping)

Topping
  • 237 ml heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 357 g fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar (to taste)

Steps


  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line the bottom of an 20 cm round pan with parchment paper and grease the sides.
  2. In a bowl, combine flour, baking powder, sugar and salt.
  3. Cut the cold butter into small cubes and rub into the dry ingredients until crumbly.
  4. Add milk and vanilla. Mix just until combined.
  5. Spread batter evenly into the prepared pan. Sprinkle with demerara sugar if using.
  6. Bake for about 30–35 minutes, until a knife inserted comes out mostly clean.
  7. Cool in the pan for 15 minutes, then turn out onto a rack and cool completely.
  8. Mix sliced strawberries with sugar and set aside to macerate.
  9. Whip cream with vanilla until soft peaks form.
  10. Top the cooled cake with whipped cream and strawberries just before serving.
