Triple Chocolate

Oreo crust + chocolate cheesecake + glossy ganache — intense, creamy, and made for chocolate lovers.

Prep 20 min | Cook 1 h | Total 6 h | Servings 16 slices

Ingredients


Crust
  • 57 g unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups Oreo cookie crumbs (about 20 Oreos)
  • Pinch of salt

Cake
  • 3 x 226 g cream cheese (room temperature)
  • 227 g sour cream (room temperature)
  • 250 g granulated sugar
  • 22 g Dutch-processed cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp espresso powder
  • 4 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 227 g bittersweet chocolate, melted

Ganache
  • 170 g bittersweet chocolate, finely chopped
  • 178 ml heavy cream

Steps


  1. Preheat the oven to 160°C. Line the bottom of a 23–25 cm springform pan with parchment paper and grease the sides.
  2. Make the crust: mix Oreo crumbs, melted butter and salt. Press firmly into the bottom of the pan. Set aside.
  3. Place a tray with hot water on the lower rack of the oven (steam helps prevent cracks).
  4. Beat cream cheese, sour cream and sugar on medium for about 2 minutes until smooth.
  5. Add cocoa, espresso powder and vanilla. Mix briefly.
  6. Add eggs one at a time, mixing just until combined (don’t overmix).
  7. Pour in the melted chocolate and mix until smooth.
  8. Pour batter over the crust. Tap the pan gently to release air bubbles.
  9. Bake for about 55 minutes, rotating halfway. The edges should be set and the center slightly wobbly.
  10. Turn off the oven and leave the door slightly open. Let cool in the oven for 1 hour.
  11. Remove, run a knife around the edge, cool 1 hour at room temperature, then cover and chill overnight.
  12. Make ganache: heat cream until very hot (not boiling). Pour over chopped chocolate, wait 1 minute, then whisk smooth.
  13. Pour ganache over the chilled cheesecake and let it set in the fridge for 10 minutes before serving.
