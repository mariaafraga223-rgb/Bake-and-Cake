Hazelnut Chocolate Tarts

Mini tarts with cocoa crust, salted caramel, toasted hazelnuts and a glossy chocolate ganache.

Prep 25 min | Cook 20 min | Total 1 h 30 min | Servings 6 mini tarts

Ingredients


Crust
  • 180 g graham cracker crumbs
  • 22 g unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar
  • 8 tbsp butter, melted

Caramel
  • 100 g granulated sugar
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp heavy cream
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 58 g roasted hazelnuts, roughly chopped

Ganache
  • 79 ml heavy cream
  • 113 g dark chocolate (60%), chopped
  • Flaky sea salt (optional, to finish)

Steps


  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Place 6 mini tart shells on a baking sheet.
  2. Make the crust: mix crumbs, cocoa, brown sugar and melted butter until combined.
  3. Press crust firmly into tart shells. Bake 10–12 minutes, then cool completely.
  4. Make the caramel: in a small saucepan over low heat, melt the sugar, stirring occasionally, 8–10 minutes.
  5. Off heat, whisk in butter, then add salt and heavy cream. Cool until warm (not hot).
  6. Spoon caramel into each tart (almost to the top). Sprinkle in most of the hazelnuts (reserve ~2 tbsp).
  7. Freeze tarts 10 minutes to set the caramel.
  8. Finely chop the reserved hazelnuts and set aside.
  9. Make ganache: heat 79 ml cream until scalding, pour over chopped chocolate and rest 5 minutes.
  10. Whisk until smooth and glossy.
  11. Spoon ganache over the chilled tarts. Top with reserved hazelnuts and flaky salt if desired.
